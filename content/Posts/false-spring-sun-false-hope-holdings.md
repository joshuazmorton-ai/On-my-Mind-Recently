title: "False Spring Sun, False Hope Holdings"
date: 2026-03-21
draft: false
---

Skipping math to enjoy weather.  
Walk with geese, collect dropped feathers.  
Staying, staring at whitewater,  
I was in love with a daughter.  
Thought I’d get to show her a city I hate.  

Some kids explore the grass.  
They’re taught by bugs, I’m taught by class.  
I held onto my wonder  
cause it would always feel like summer,  
now it only feels like the weight of not knowing.  

Texting my friends to come and join me,  
it’s real nice but I’m still lonely.  
But I went back to eat my breakfast,  
Role Model’s slipfast  
played off my phone speakers.  

Convince myself my life's romantic,  
but I’m the sun over Europe’s side of the Atlantic.  
Nothing wrong with being solitary  
when I’m just too much to carry.  
So I’ll settle down like Buckley, underwater.  

But I’ve been trying to find peace,  
Uncle Sam said it’s up for lease.  
But the least that I can do  
is not talk to you,  
so I’ll skip class with the geese.
