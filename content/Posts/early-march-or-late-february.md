title: "Early March or Late February"
date: 2026-03-21
draft: false
---

When the sun through a window  
seems deceivingly warm,  
and you wake like a widow  
needing newness, a beehive swarm.  

Back when the sundials shadow shrouded  
all the time I had left, and the clock  
struck only those clouded,  
only those unknown falling flocks.  

And there was nothing to do  
but but something to know,  
something hidden in a man of glue  
melting under the sun, flying near just for show.  

When men would melt and mold,  
knowing they must destroy to create.  
Folding for ideas, only what they were told.  
Cyclic news, it circulates.
