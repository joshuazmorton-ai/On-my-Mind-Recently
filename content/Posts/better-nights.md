title: "Better Nights"
date: 2026-03-21
draft: false
---

In the morning you always feel better.  
Starting new, starting better  
than the days you need a reminder "it will get better."  
Awake to the sun, the light treats you better  
than the dark nights that never get better.   
Make eggs, make coffee -- routine makes you better.  
And yet I pour the pot and it stops getting better.  

You made my only mug so I can't drink without knowing it's you I'm misssing.  
I quit drinking coffee, my lips on the glass, too close to us kissing.   

In the evening your eyes paint the sky called night  
even my tinnitus hums your name at night.  
I jog the canal to outrun the night,   
but its not a thing I can blame on the night.  
So when I lay in bed to quicken the night,  
I lie with the one that hides in the night.  
I wish it was you, not me, who speaks at night.   

The words come from a mind thats peeling, and when the feeling starts peaking,   
I write in my notes, it's the closest thing to us speaking.
