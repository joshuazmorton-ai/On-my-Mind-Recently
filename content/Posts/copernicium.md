A belt on a pull-up bar.  
A note on a desk.  
A mind without brakes.  

Its too easy to lose touch,  
nowadays I’m not even with myself much.  
And every choice seems to attack,  
so I love you but I won’t text back.  

And the chemical people visit instead.  
only sadness held my hand when I was down.  
That sad blue girl stopped the blood that shed,  
so it's okay my friends aren't around.  

Dim clothes and a stone hat,  
my legacy is making worms fat.  
I hate it here, and can’t go home,  
but I’m scared to leave only blood and bone.  

I am not an iron, I am copernicium.  
I’ll tie a rope to the fields of Elysium.  
I’ll let myself be lost to the lethe,  
as my eyes watch my soul fall far beneath me.
