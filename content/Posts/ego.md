title: "Ego"
date: 2026-03-21
draft: false
---

When I was a child,  
small evil men lived under my bed.  
I couldn’t sleep  
because of tears I shed.  
My sword: a flashlight;  
I went to kill,  
and realised my fears  
were just a thrill.  

Nothing was ever there.  

And now as a man,  
I find some stranger in my mind.  
moving the parts of me  
that once were only kind.  
I went to the mirror to meet him,  
the man controlling the shots.  
To ask him where he came from.  
But in the light he was nought.  

Nothing was ever there.
