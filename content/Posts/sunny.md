---
title: "Sunny"
date: 2026-03-21
draft: false
---

Only the sun has held me without letting go,  
yet she latched onto me in a warm embrace.  
Her rays kissing my cheek and neck.  

So when the clouds came to shroud her ray,  
I rushed to become wind to blow them away.  
Not just so the rays could reach me,  
but also to see her shine.  

But I blew til I pushed her into nightfall,  
I felt her drowning under the horizon line,  
burning the floor beneath me.
