🌐 On my Mind, Recently 🌐

By Joshua Zhì Chéng Morton

Hello friends!

Fuck this is sick as hell I have a website guys. If you don’t know me, my name is Joshua Morton. I’m a writer, mostly poetry, and I created this site to share some of my writings. I have a book, titled Notes on a Quarter Life Crisis, but for many of these poems I wanted to share them in a more subtle manner. I think as a person I’ve been growing quieter, so the appeal to releasing books is leaving me. I don’t want these poems to be read much, they’re both more personal to me and holding less objective meaning. But I still wish to share my work in case it could give an emotion to someone, hence this project: On my Mind, Recently. I hope you enjoy these little scenes from my eyes, the moments I hope to capture in my writing.
