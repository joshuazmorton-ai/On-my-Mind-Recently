Splitting

📅 Published: Saturday, March 21, 2026 📖 1 min read 📊 102 words

Ribs five and six split on the side
and all of my good traits are lost outside.
I hear my heart under the floorboards.
I want to die when I’m bored,
so I went outside and almost cried
but I got lucky cause there’s people around.
I heard the patio creaking
and wrote in my notes – it’s the closest you you and I speaking.

Am I good? Am I worthless?
I don’t want to worry about it anymore.
I listen for battalions and imagine death in war
and I quote Bataille’s Ecstasy and walk between cars.

Guess I’m guilty after all.
