On my Mind, Recently
Lei Niho
_ □ ×

Lei Niho

📅 Published: Saturday, March 21, 2026 📖 1 min read 📊 109 words

A necklace of ivory, beads to be borne.
Stuck behind glass, a beauty not worn.
It piqued my attention as I walked in the room,
and I stared, longing that it was buried in my tomb.

Those beautiful things one can wear:
a mask of selfhood for one to bear.
The prettiest things that I so desire,
is it a way for me to conspire becoming a liar?

I stare in the display at what I can’t touch,
I draw near to those traits, those I never had much.
The beauty of a necklace, same as smiles in friends.
Attribute them to me, it’s my means to an end.
