I still dont know

📅 Published: Saturday, March 21, 2026 📖 2 min read 📊 218 words

I dont like to read books that I havent bought
and I like to buy books I wont read.
I like to find tangents, find things I’ve been taught
and I like to do all the things I dont need.

I stare at my phone like I’m looking through glasses
and when I get stressed I just skip all my classes.
I talked to my friends, they don’t know what I mean
I would talk to a shrink, but I need to get clean.

I read all the books,
I dont know what they mean.
And I keep seeing views,
I dont know what they mean.
And I trust my thoughts,
it’s the language I don’t.
So I guess I dont know what they mean.

I’ll call up my closest, we can work as a team.
And I’ll pray I’m no statistic, requiem for a dream.
But joy always flees me and I never know why.
I dont know if it’s moral to lay down and die.

It used to all make sense to me, at least I felt I understood but im losing my capacity to comprehend the things I should. Do I find peace or be the best of me, I can only pick one. The best needs my ego, but at peace I need none.
