Dramatics

📅 Published: Saturday, March 21, 2026 📖 2 min read 📊 249 words

How can you tell between hurt and dramatics?
Real pain or some defense — acrobatics
across the hurt i’ve caused.
When can I ask for help if the world paused?

Bailing on friends, skipping appointments.
I know I haven’t been well,
I know my scars need an ointment.
I know I shouldn’t be guilty of needing a hand,
I know it isn’t right to interrupt everyone’s plans.

Someone read my mind and find the heart
to ask me if I need to talk to someone.
I’d say I don’t and hide in the sun.
I’ll say we should just go have some fun,
but I’m staying in tonight cause a hand is a gun.

But I’ll make those plans when I’m manic
so now im guilty and in panic.
I’ll take these emotions and make crappy poetry,
my words used to be so kind, now they’re just a leaking misery.

I don’t want to bring it up.
I don’t want to leave this place.
But I can’t even identify with my face.
But what if this hurt is excuses out of place
and its karma that wraps my neck in a lace.

Do I deserve it, I don’t know.
I have to believe that I don’t.
I have to believe that I don’t.

And I feel it again, beginning to start,
I don’t even know what played a part.
But pain grows where reality is denied.
But I believe and I try, I try to know where I lied.
