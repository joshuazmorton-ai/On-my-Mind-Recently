Prince of Denmark

📅 Published: Saturday, March 21, 2026 📖 1 min read 📊 151 words

I know I have potential
yet I rot in residentials.
Until I know just who I am,
I’ll define myself by my credentials.

Do tears after a kill
show you that you still can change?
Individualism is a krill,
Ego burrows as mange.

How do you take your life so seriously?
I was a child, unknown, the inchworms and me.
I still wonder what it’s like to find
if purpose is real or a means to fill time.

So drown me in accolades
for the rest of the world to praise.
When everyone loves me
there will be no dismay.

So I will keep running to the next
and I will not succumb to the lexical limitations
and other hexes upon me I
will never know about.

And they once called me a great light.
Now I shift shapes to a chandelier,
hanging from the ceiling.
And I was never there.
