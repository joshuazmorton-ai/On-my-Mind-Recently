Pursuits on Peace

📅 Published: Saturday, March 21, 2026 📖 1 min read 📊 105 words

We went shopping for food.
You said something about your mood,
but reflected and said something smart.
Then you hit a wall with the cart.

You stared at my eyes without any motion
and we took too long and went to the ocean.
You always talk about the sea, but only saw the waves
through your phone camera, next to a video of your last raves.

You race to read the right quotes,
quoting Annata like it’s your name on your coat.
Racing to enlightenment, it’s your next big achievement but for now you whine.
And when you finally found peace you posted it online.
